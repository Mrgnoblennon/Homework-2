Hello! my name is Chase Barrett-Brown. This webpage was developed to showcase my progressive understanding of combining HTML and CSS apects to further my knowledge in webpage development and design.


This Webpage has three unique features. Within the navigation bar to the right, you can see four words. Three of these words correspond with another section on the page and so when clicked, the webpage's view will be scrolled to the heading in the left most margin.


Another feature used is a pseudo class, demonstrated when the mouse cursor is hovering above any of the images below. The images start off transparent and once hovered, the images should smoothly and quickly transition to completely opaque and back to transparent when the element has been un-hovered. Also when only the titles of the images are clicked, you will be directedt to another webpage in a new tab.


Lastly, media queries have been incorporated within the style sheet to adjust pixel size for different devices such as laptops, tablets or smartphones.
